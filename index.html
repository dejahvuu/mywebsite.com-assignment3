Plate to Palette (PTP)

Welcome to Plate to Palette (PTP), where nourishment fuels creativity. We are a local non-profit dedicated to supporting young artists by providing meals, fostering creativity, and building a strong, united community. At PTP, we believe that no dream should be hindered by hunger. Through our programs, we offer not just food, but a safe and inspiring space where youth can connect, express themselves, and grow. Whether you're a young artist in need of support or someone looking to make a difference, there's a place for you at PTP. If you're seeking meals and a creative community, you can sign up to participate in our programs. If you're passionate about giving back, we welcome volunteers to help serve meals, mentor youth, or assist with events. Together, we can nourish both body and soul, creating a future where every young artist has the strength to pursue their dreams.

Make Your Mark

At PTP, we're committed to giving young artists the support they need to thrive. Our Nourish & Create Program provides free meals and art classes to youth in need, ensuring they have both the strength and the creative space to follow their passions. Whether it's a warm meal, a place to express themselves, or a community that uplifts them, we're here to help.

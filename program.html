Why Nourish & Create

At Nourish & Create, we believe that creativity thrives when basic needs are met. Our program offers free meals to young
adults (18-24) in the GTA, ensuring they have the fuel they need to thrive, both physically and mentally. We understand that
hunger can be a barrier to focusing on dreams, so we provide a safe and welcoming space where individuals can access
nutritious food and explore their creativity without worry.

But we don't stop at food—we also offer workshops, mentorship, and opportunities for personal growth. Whether you're
looking to develop artistic skills, connect with others, or discover new passions, Nourish & Create is a place where you can grow
in every way. It's more than just a meal—it's a community of support and opportunity for young adults to unleash their
potential and build a brighter future.

VOLUNTEER WITH PTP - Make a Difference!

At PTP, we believe in the power of community and the impact that volunteers can have in shaping the lives of young adults. By volunteering with us, you're not just helping to serve meals—you're helping to create a safe, supportive space where young people can thrive. Whether you're mentoring, assisting with workshops, or simply providing a friendly face, your time and skills will make a lasting difference in the lives of those we serve. We're currently offering volunteer positions such as art teacher, cook, and server. Join our dedicated team of volunteers and help us nourish and inspire the next generation!

How to Sign Up as a Volunteer:

1. Fill out the Volunteer Application Form - Provide your contact information, availability, and areas of interest.

2. Complete a Background Check - Ensure the safety and wellbeing of our participants.

3. Attend an Orientation - Learn about PTP's mission, guidelines, and how you can get involved.

4. Start Volunteering! - Once approved, you'll be ready to contribute and make a positive impact in the community.

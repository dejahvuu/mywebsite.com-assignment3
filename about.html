Our Mission

At Plate to Palette (PTP), our mission is to ensure that no young adult has to choose between food and their future. We are dedicated to nourishing both body and creativity by providing free meals, a safe space, and artistic opportunities for young adults (18-24) in the Greater Toronto Area. Through food security and creative expression, we aim to uplift and empower individuals, fostering a supportive community where everyone has the chance to thrive.

Founded in 2004, PTP began as a grassroots initiative to provide meals to young artists struggling to balance their passion with financial hardship. What started as a small meal-sharing program quickly grew into a space where young people could connect, create, and find support beyond just food.

Over the years, we have collaborated with organizations such as the Art Gallery of Ontario (AGO), local food banks, and community art programs to expand our reach and provide even greater opportunities for young adults.

Today, PTP serves hundreds of young adults each year, offering nutritious meals, free art workshops, and mentorship programs designed to inspire and uplift. We provide a welcoming environment where young people can explore their passions—whether they are artists, musicians, writers, or simply individuals seeking a safe and supportive space. Our Nourish & Create program has helped countless participants regain confidence, build friendships, and discover new opportunities through food and artistic expression.

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our reach, ensuring that no young adult in the GTA goes hungry or feels isolated.

Our goals include:
- Increasing the number of meals served per year
- Expanding our volunteer network to provide more mentorship and creative guidance
- Establishing more accessible locations for PTP programming
- Developing career and skill-building workshops alongside our creative programs

Through our work, we strive to create lasting change—not just by feeding individuals for a day, but by providing them with the tools, resources, and community to build a sustainable future. At PTP, we believe that when young people are nourished, their creativity and potential know no bounds.

The Team Behind PTP

Alex Chen - Program Director

With a background in social work, Alex co-founded PTP in 2004 to ensure young adults never have to choose between food and their future. His leadership has helped PTP grow from a small initiative into a thriving non-profit that nourishes and empowers hundreds each year.

Samantha Reyes - Artist & Educator

As an artist and former educator, Samantha saw how financial barriers held young creatives back. She co-founded PTP to provide free meals and artistic opportunities, helping launch the Nourish & Create program in partnership with organizations like the AGO.

Jordan Brown - Culinary Expert

With a background in culinary arts, Jordan helped shape PTP's food program, ensuring every participant receives nutritious, high-quality meals. His passion for food as a tool for connection has led to partnerships with local restaurants and food banks.
